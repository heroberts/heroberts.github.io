Dr. Hannah Roberts

Hi! I am a postdoctoral researcher at the Department of Human Geography and Spatial Planning, Utrecht Unviersity. I work on the “NEEDS” project (Dynamic Urban Environmental Exposures on Depression and Suicide). More specifically, I examine the association between depression and the environment along people’s daily space-time paths. More information on the project is available here.
